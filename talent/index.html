About
TERON Talent Management

Social media is best to talk to your existing and prospective customers, engage with those who will promote your brand. It’s also one of the most rapidly changing marketing channels, with new features and updates constantly being released by Facebook, Twitter, Instagram. It can be hard to keep up, or even know where to start! That’s the job of our social media team who have all the experience needed to make the most of your profiles – whatever the nature of your business.







OUR Aim

Shine bright, you diamond, you!

You have a voice that is distinct and worth listening to. We are committed to not pigeonholing you or assigning you a category as an influencer. No really, we hate that. So, we really get to know you, and this translates into some pretty amazing brand collaborations that are truly customized to empower you to be part of some high quality experiences.

A platform for you, by you

Have an idea? We will work with you to package and deliver it to decision makers in brands across the country. We have access to and deep relationships with brands in every conceivable category and domain.

Be a cool cat and collab!

We work with some other amazing storytellers like you. And sometimes, two, or four are better than one. We’ll connect you with other influencers across genres to help you collaborate on projects that will provide you with a platform to tell your stories.

You’re worth a pretty penny!

We know, how do you communicate the value you bring beyond just your follower count? Teron understands better than most how important it is for brands and partners to realise that you bring a whole lot more to the table than your reach. Your brand, voice, and value as a storyteller is the real deal in any partnership, and we’re here to help you hone in on a number that represents everything you are as an influencer.
